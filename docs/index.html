Labor Cost Lab

The Labor Cost Lab is an open-access research platform designed to compare labour cost structures, net wages and social contribution systems across countries.

First national module

The French Labour Cost Lab is the first operational module of the Labor Cost Lab project. It focuses on the French wage-cost system and the interaction between the minimum wage, employer contribution reductions and marginal labour cost dynamics.
